What is a Particle?

Particles in DOTS Dynamic Bone is a data structure that represents a Bone within a model i.e: a Hip, Finger, Hair, or Tail bone. The Particles are updated and modified during the simulation based on the Particle and DOTSDynamicBone parameters. Particles also can represent different things like segments, or divisions within a model. During each update, data from the associated DOTSDynamicBone is used to update the Particles in an initalization-like step. Afterward, the Particles is put through different methods that update its position and rotation based on the input data.

Where are Particles Created?

Particles are created during baking using the DOTSDynamicBone.CreateParticles.
